Hyderabad Metro Rail is an online web portal where a passenger can know the fare for travelling through metro based on the distance in kilometers

Metro Rates tab gives the passenger information about the rates from one place to another based on the travel distance The parking rates for different types of vechiles can be viewed in Parking Rates tab

The smart card can be recharged using the Recharge tab

Coupon codes can be applied to avail a discount while travelling through the smart cards. The codes will be valid for a certain period of time and can be found in Coupons tab

Passengers can also change their passwords by clicking on the settings menu where they may find 'Change password' option.

Passengers can edit their general details by clicking on the 'Edit Profile' option available in the settings menu.
